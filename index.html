Beta 1.5 (Save v8)
Old World-Inspired Systems
- Orders system: limited actions per turn (base 3 + 1/city + legitimacy bonus)
- First action per unit costs 1 order; subsequent moves with that unit are free
- AI also uses orders for balanced gameplay

Legitimacy
- New resource (0-100) shown in topbar with fleur-de-lis
- Increases from cities, winning battles, capturing cities, and event choices
- Decreases from losing units, losing cities, and event choices
- Affects max orders per turn and score

Narrative Events
- 12 Napoleonic-era events with meaningful choices every 4 turns
- Events affect resources and legitimacy
- Includes: Bountiful Harvest, Desertion, Traveling Merchant, Provincial Unrest, and more

Bug Fixes
- Fixed defeat screen showing "undefined" for military count
- Keyboard hotkeys and camera no longer work during event modals
Beta 1.4 (Save v8)
Gameplay
- Rest & Heal (H key): wounded units can rest to heal +3 HP (+5 in cities), uses full turn
- Resting also fortifies the unit

Victory & Defeat
- Full-screen game over overlay when your empire falls
- Full-screen victory overlay with score breakdown
- Fixed defeat screen showing wrong territory count
Beta 1.3 (Save v8)
Combat Overhaul
- Terrain defense bonuses: Forest +25%, Mountain +35%, Swamp +15%, City +40%
- Fortify units (V key) for +20% defense bonus; auto-fortifies if stationary
- Siege units (cannons, mortars, field guns) deal +50% damage vs cities
- Melee kills no longer teleport attacker across impassable terrain

Diplomacy Rebalance
- Loyalty now drifts toward neutral instead of constantly decaying to war
- Stronger trade and shared-enemy bonuses for stable alliances
- AI forms coalitions against dominant empires (40%+ cities)
- War warnings: toasts at Hostile and Imminent thresholds

Economy
- Unit maintenance: military units cost 0.5 gold/turn (2 free per city)
- Gold rate in top bar now shows net income after maintenance

AI
- Diversified army composition (AI builds mix of available unit types)
- Emergency production: AI cancels civilian builds under heavy threat

Bug Fixes
- Fixed AI ranged attacks ignoring terrain and fortification defense
- Fixed movement animation playing backwards
- Fixed barbarian camp respawn timer ignoring configured delay
- Fixed tutorial replaying every new game after being skipped
- Fixed peace toggle not clearing stale peace offers in UI

Beta 1.0 (Save v8)
Sound Effects
- Marching SFX plays during unit movement animations
- Musket shots for musketeers, riflemen, dragoons
- Cannon volleys for cannons and mortars (multiple synchronized shots)
- Field gun volleys for field artillery
- Naval broadside volleys for galleons and men-of-war
- Town ambient sounds near cities and urban tiles (like forest birds)
- Combat sounds only play for battles the player can see

Deep Diplomacy
- AI thinks like a ruler: evaluates city proximity, military threats, territory encroachment
- Happiness decays -0.5 per turn (trust erodes naturally)
- Cities within 12 tiles cause -9 happiness (border tension)
- Military buildup near borders increases suspicion
- Territory encroachment near AI cities angers them
- Power imbalance: much stronger neighbors feel threatening
- Shared enemies build trust (+2), attacking friends causes distrust
- Trade bonus while at peace with cities (+0.3/turn)
- War declared at -100 happiness (was 0)
- Declaring war immediately sets target to Hostile
- New loyalty tiers: Cautious, Sworn Enemy

UI & Settings

Beta 0.9 (Save v8)
Balance
- Unit HP now varies by type (grenadiers tanky, ranged units fragile)
- Late-game unit production costs reduced ~25-30%
- Damage scales with attacker HP (wounded units hit softer)
- Combat units heal +2/turn in own territory, +3 in cities

Barbarian Camps
- Camps spawn on unexplored territory after turn 5 (40% chance/turn)
- Axemen raid and pillage your improvements
- Place a unit on a camp to destroy it (respawns in 10 turns)
- Workers can repair pillaged buildings within 2 turns
- Settle on camps or city sites to found new cities

Win Conditions
- Score system: cities, territory, techs, military, conquests
- Domination victory: eliminate all rivals
- Score victory at turn 200: highest score wins
- Victory/defeat screens with full score breakdown

- Wounded AI units retreat to heal
- AI prioritizes high-value targets in combat
- Dynamic expansion based on available sites and military
- Strategic tech selection (military when at war, economy in peace)
- Threat-aware production: builds army when enemies are near
- AI offers peace treaties (accept/reject in Kingdoms panel)
- AI units no longer get stuck on coastlines
