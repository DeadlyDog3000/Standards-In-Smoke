Beta 1.0 (Save v8)
Sound Effects
- Marching SFX plays during unit movement animations
- Musket shots for musketeers, riflemen, dragoons
- Cannon volleys for cannons and mortars (multiple synchronized shots)
- Field gun volleys for field artillery
- Naval broadside volleys for galleons and men-of-war
- Town ambient sounds near cities and urban tiles (like forest birds)
- Combat sounds only play for battles the player can see

Deep Diplomacy
- AI thinks like a ruler: evaluates city proximity, military threats, territory encroachment
- Happiness decays -0.5 per turn (trust erodes naturally)
- Cities within 12 tiles cause -9 happiness (border tension)
- Military buildup near borders increases suspicion
- Territory encroachment near AI cities angers them
- Power imbalance: much stronger neighbors feel threatening
- Shared enemies build trust (+2), attacking friends causes distrust
- Trade bonus while at peace with cities (+0.3/turn)
- War declared at -100 happiness (was 0)
- Declaring war immediately sets target to Hostile
- New loyalty tiers: Cautious, Sworn Enemy

UI & Settings
- Calligraphy font throughout the game
- Large italicized title on main menu
- Barbarian camp density slider in game setup (default 60%)
Beta 0.9 (Save v8)
Balance
- Unit HP now varies by type (grenadiers tanky, ranged units fragile)
- Late-game unit production costs reduced ~25-30%
- Damage scales with attacker HP (wounded units hit softer)
- Combat units heal +2/turn in own territory, +3 in cities
- Science generation increased, science labs cheaper

Barbarian Camps
- Camps spawn on unexplored territory after turn 5 (40% chance/turn)
- Axemen raid and pillage your improvements
- Place a unit on a camp to destroy it (respawns in 10 turns)
- Workers can repair pillaged buildings within 2 turns
- Settle on camps or city sites to found new cities

Win Conditions
- Score system: cities, territory, techs, military, conquests
- Domination victory: eliminate all rivals
- Score victory at turn 200: highest score wins
- Victory/defeat screens with full score breakdown

Smarter AI
- AI garrisons undefended cities in peacetime
- Wounded AI units retreat to heal
- AI prioritizes high-value targets in combat
- Dynamic expansion based on available sites and military
- Strategic tech selection (military when at war, economy in peace)
- Threat-aware production: builds army when enemies are near
- AI offers peace treaties (accept/reject in Kingdoms panel)
- AI units no longer get stuck on coastlines

UI
- Attack power and unit stats shown in panels
- Turn counter and score in top bar
- Military strength and score in Kingdoms panel
- Tile yield info in tooltips
- Hotkey reference panel
- Naval units can now use go-to orders on water
- Camera waits for movement animation before cycling to next unit
- Science lab can now be built with just 1 city (was requiring 2)
- Compact save format reduces save size ~60%
- Delete buttons on save slots to free storage
- Fixed ESC key causing browser zoom
- Update log shown on first launch per version
